# Emergency Change Approval

Emergency changes are deployed to production outside the standard change window when service is at risk. Because they bypass pre-approval, each emergency change must be captured in the change log and approved retrospectively by the Change Advisory Board (CAB), and evidence that the change was tested after implementation must be retained. This limits the risk that unauthorised or defective changes persist in production unnoticed.

## Control Attributes
* Every emergency change is recorded as a ticket in the emergency change log
* Retrospective CAB approval is obtained within 5 business days of deployment
* Post-implementation testing evidence is retained for every emergency change
